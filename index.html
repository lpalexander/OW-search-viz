Interacting with the map

  • Use the 'time of day' slider to view average daily ONE>WAY vehicle searches in half-hour time windows
  • Use the 'animate' button to view searches beginning at the selected 'time of day' and ending after 24 hours, or when the 'animate' button is pressed again
  • Use the 'day of week' radio buttons to view average daily ONE>WAY vehicle searches for weekdays or weekends
  • Click a hexbin to see the number of average daily searches in a given area and time
  • Zooming the map in and out will result in smaller and larger hexbins, respectively
  • To explore areas not presently shown in the map, click to pan in any direction

Understanding the map

  • Hexbin hue reflects the number of searches relative to other hexbins in a given time of day and day of week
  • The darker the hexbin hue, the higher the demand
  • Search data spans the 18-week period between 7/6/2015 and 11/8/2015
  • The green circles reflect the locations of ONE>WAY parking spaces on 9/7/2015
